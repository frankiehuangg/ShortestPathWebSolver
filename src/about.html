About Page

Welcome to our website! We are two students currently pursuing an informatics major who are passionate about solving complex problems using algorithms and data structures.

This website is dedicated to helping people find the shortest path from a set of points on a map. We understand that finding the fastest or most efficient way to travel is important for many reasons such as saving time reducing transportation costs or avoiding traffic. That's why we have developed algorithms that can find the shortest path quickly and accurately.

We seek to provide our users with the most accurate and efficient tools for finding the shortest path on a map. We believe that technology can make our lives easier and more convenient and we are dedicated to leveraging the latest advances in computer science to achieve that goal.

We are committed to providing the highest level of customer service and support. If you have any questions or comments please don't hesitate to contact us. We are always happy to help and we welcome any feedback that can help us improve our website and algorithms.

Thank you for visiting our website and we hope that you find our tools and resources helpful in your journey to find the shortest path on a map.
